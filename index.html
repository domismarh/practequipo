Why Dont We

Why Don't We (commonly abbreviated as WDW) is an American boy band. Consisting of five members, Zach Herron, Jack Avery, Daniel Seavey, Corbyn Besson, and Jonah Marais, the band was formed on September 27, 2016. Each of the members had solo careers prior to joining the group.

Corbyn Besson.


Corbyn Besson (born November 25, 1998) was born in Dallas, Texas, and raised in Virginia. Besson's cousins are Dutch girl group O'G3NE. Before Why Don't We, he had a following on YouNow and released a solo single called "The Only One" to iTunes in 2014. Besson is dating YouTuber Christina Marie.

Daniel Seavey.



Daniel Seavey (born April 2, 1999) grew up in Vancouver, Washington. He can play over 20 instruments by ear. As a child, his father would take him to Portland, Oregon to do street performances on the city's art walks. In 2015, Seavey competed on season 14 of American Idol and finished in ninth place. He accomplished this at age 15, making him the youngest finalist of the season.

Zach Herron.



Zach Herron (born May 27, 2001) was raised in Dallas, Texas. Growing up, he sang in choir. Before Why Don't We, Herron covered songs on YouTube. His cover of Stitches by Shawn Mendes went viral. He was part of the meet-and-greet tour "Impact", which Avery was also a part of.

Jonah Marais.



Jonah Marais (born June 16, 1998) grew up in Stillwater, Minnesota. Before Why Don't We, Marais had a following on YouNow. He has also released an album, When the Daylight's Gone, in 2016 and went on the 2014 DigiTour.

Jack Avery.



Jack Avery (born July 1, 1999) was born in Burbank, California, but raised in Susquehanna, Pennsylvania. Avery released a solo single called "Liar" in 2016 and was part of the meet-and-greet tour "Impact", which Herron was also a part of. He also starred in a short film called Fearless Five. On April 22, 2019, Avery's girlfriend, Gabriela Gonzalez, gave birth to their daughter, Lavender May Avery
